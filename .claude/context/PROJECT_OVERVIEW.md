# Project Overview

## Purpose

AI Insights Daily is a fully automated AI blog that publishes daily articles about artificial intelligence news and trends. The goal is to generate passive income through platform monetization while building an audience in the AI/tech space.

**Owner:** Akilan, Chennai, India  
**Started:** May 2026  
**Status:** Live and running

---

## Target Audience

- Software developers and engineers interested in AI
- Tech enthusiasts following AI developments
- Business professionals tracking AI adoption
- Students learning about machine learning and AI

**Tone:** Conversational but professional. Accessible to intermediate tech readers, not just experts.

---

## Publishing Platforms

| Platform | Audience | Monetization | Status |
|----------|----------|--------------|--------|
| Dev.to | Developers | None (exposure) | ✅ Auto |
| Hashnode | Developers | None (exposure) | ✅ Auto |
| Medium | General tech | Partner Program $ | 🔶 Manual (30 sec) |

---

## Revenue Model

**Current (passive):**
- Medium Partner Program: ~$0.01-0.10 per read (varies wildly)
- Estimated: $0.05/post × 30 posts/month = ~$1.50/month starting point
- Grows with audience: at 10K monthly reads → ~$50-200/month

**Future (planned):**
- Sponsored posts (AI tools, SaaS products)
- Newsletter (Substack or ConvertKit)
- Affiliate links (AI tools, courses)
- Digital products (templates, guides)

---

## Content Strategy

**Topic focus:** AI news, tools, trends, tutorials, industry analysis  
**Post length:** 800-1000 words  
**Frequency:** Daily (7 days/week)  
**Style rotation (daily angle):**
- Developer implications
- Business impact
- Society & ethics
- Technical deep-dive
- Future predictions
- Risks & controversies
- Startups & disruptors

**Content source:** 5 RSS feeds (TechCrunch AI, The Verge AI, VentureBeat AI, MIT Tech Review, Hacker News)

## Success Metrics

**Weekly checks:**
- Posts published this week (target: 7/7)
- Platform success rate (target: 100%)
- Email delivery rate (target: 100%)

**Monthly checks:**
- Total posts cumulative
- Medium Partner Program earnings
- Dev.to follower growth
- Hashnode follower growth

**Quality checks:**
- Word count per post (target: 800-1000)
- Post uniqueness (no duplicate titles/angles)
- Tag relevance score

## Long-Term Vision

Build AI Insights Daily into a trusted daily resource for AI news, growing from automated content to a hybrid model where:
1. Automation handles daily news summaries (current)
2. Manual deep-dives added weekly (future)
3. Community grows around the content
4. Monetization scales with audience

**3-year vision:** 50K monthly readers, $500-2000/month passive income from writing.
